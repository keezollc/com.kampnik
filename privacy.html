Keezo Privacy Policy

Effective on March 16, 2015

This privacy policy describes how Keezo LLC (Keezo) collects and uses information from its Kampnik app and website software.

We may collect the following personal information from you:

  • Usage information about how you interact with our software.
  • Device information which may include IP address and serial number.

We may use this information to:

Improve our software by analyzing how it is being used.

We may share this information:

As required by law, to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process.

Information you provide us:

For our Kampnik software, any information you submit such as campground details or errors, we may share that information with the owner of the http://www.USCAmpgrounds.info website. Keezo uses that website’s campground data in Kampnik, therefore sharing campground information with that website improves the software we can provide to you.

Third party services:

Keezo uses the Google Analytics service to evaluate the stability and usage of all of our software. Some of the information collected by this service includes: Device type, network, and location as well as usage tracking and crash or exception reports. Keezo has no control over how Google manages or uses information that is collected, therefore you should review their privacy policy to learn more - http://www.google.com/policies/privacy.

Third party links:

Links to external websites are in all of our software. Keezo has no control over your privacy once you leave our software by accessing a website via link. You should consult the third party website privacy policy to determine how your privacy is affected. Therefore we have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of the linked website.

Notification of changes:

Keezo may update this privacy policy at any time. Policy updates will be posted to our website http://www.keezo.co or downloaded as an update to our software. You are encouraged to check our website for the latest privacy policy information.
