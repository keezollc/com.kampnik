Keezo has no control over how Google manages or uses information that is collected, therefore you should review their privacy policy to learn more. Google Privacy Policy.

Links to external websites are in all of our software. Keezo has no control over your privacy once you leave our software by accessing a website via link. You should consult the third party website privacy policy to determine how your privacy is affected. Therefore we have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of the linked website.

Notification of changes

Keezo may update this privacy policy at any time. Policy updates will be posted to our website www.keezo.co or downloaded as an update to our software. You are encouraged to check our website for the latest privacy policy information.

Contact

You can contact Keezo about this privacy policy or any other topic via our website www.keezo.co.
